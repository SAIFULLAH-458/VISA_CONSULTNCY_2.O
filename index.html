FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does the visa process take?

Processing times vary by country and visa type. Student visas typically take 2-8 weeks, work visas 4-12 weeks, and permanent residency applications 6-18 months. We provide estimated timelines during your initial consultation.

What is your success rate?

We maintain a 99.2% visa approval rate across all visa categories. Our thorough assessment ensures we only take on cases with strong approval potential.

Do you offer free initial consultations?

Yes, we offer a complimentary 15-minute preliminary assessment to understand your case and determine eligibility before you commit to a paid consultation package.

What happens if my visa is rejected?

With our Premium Package, we offer visa rejection insurance covering re-application at no extra cost. For other packages, we assist with appeals at discounted rates.

Do you help with document preparation?

Absolutely! Our Full Application and Premium packages include complete document review, preparation, translation assistance, and notarization guidance.
